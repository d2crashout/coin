Coin Flip

Welcome to PGIM Fixed Income's Coin Flip Challenge!

You are offered a virtual stake of $25 to bet on the flip of a coin for thirty minutes.

The coin is biased to come up heads with a 60% probability, and you can bet as much as you'd like on heads or tails for each flip.

The game ends either when you go bankrupt, the 30 minutes ends, or you decide to finish the game early.
